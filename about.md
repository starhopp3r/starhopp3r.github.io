Hello! I'm Nikhil, a sophomore at [Nanyang Technological University](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/Pages/home.aspx), majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. I have a strong passion for creating products that make people's lives better, with a focus on aesthetics and ingenuity. I'm a serial-hackathon attendee, and I've won several prizes from Bosch, Amazon Web Services, and Airbus Defence and Space.

I have been awarded the [Tan Kah Kee Young Inventors' Award](https://www.tkkfoundation.org.sg/young-inventors-awards) for my invention titled: Disaster Recovery with HOPE handset and COMPASS dashboard, along with my fellow inventors and buddies [James](https://codingindex.xyz) and [Bryan](https://modelconverge.xyz).

I'm an autodidact who likes to learn about computing, electronics, space, and design during my free time. I use this personal website to collect and organize my thoughts and learning experiences into blog posts and tutorials. If you like my posts, kindly share them with people interested in these topics. I hope you find the content in my blog helpful.
